Scoring System

How Every
Score is Decided

Our TF-IDF cosine similarity engine produces a 0–100 relevance score for every resume. Here's exactly what each range means for your pipeline.

Score Range Status What It Means Recruiter Action Signal Strength
75 – 100 Shortlisted Strong keyword and context overlap with the job description Schedule interview immediately
50 – 74 On Hold Partial match — candidate has some relevant skills but gaps exist Review manually, consider for other roles
0 – 49 Rejected Low semantic similarity — resume does not align with role requirements Archive; send automated rejection

RecruitAI uses TF-IDF (Term Frequency–Inverse Document Frequency) combined with cosine similarity to measure how closely a resume's language and keywords align with a given job description. Each resume receives a score from 0 to 100 — higher means stronger match. No personal data like name or gender influences the score.
